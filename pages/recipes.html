🇹🇭 My Favorite Thai Dishes

Thai cuisine is rich, flavorful, and diverse. Here are some of my all-time favorite Thai dishes, from spicy soups to delicious street food.

🍜 Noodle Dishes

  • Pad Thai – Stir-fried rice noodles with shrimp, tofu, peanuts, and tamarind sauce.
  • Pad See Ew – Flat noodles stir-fried in soy sauce with meat and Chinese broccoli.
  • Boat Noodles (Kuay Teow Reua) – Rich, dark noodle soup with beef or pork.
  • Yen Ta Fo – Pink noodle soup with fish balls and fermented bean curd.

🍛 Curry Dishes

  • Green Curry (Gaeng Keow Wan) – Spicy green coconut curry with Thai basil and meat.
  • Red Curry (Gaeng Daeng) – Spicy coconut-based curry with red chili paste.
  • Massaman Curry – Mild curry with potatoes and peanuts, influenced by Indian spices.
  • Panang Curry – Thick red curry with a slightly sweet coconut flavor.

🥗 Salads

  • Som Tum (Green Papaya Salad) – Spicy shredded papaya salad with lime and peanuts.
  • Larb – Spicy minced meat salad with mint, lime, and roasted rice powder.
  • Yum Woon Sen – Glass noodle salad with shrimp and vegetables.

🍲 Soups

  • Tom Yum Goong – Hot and sour shrimp soup with lemongrass and chili.
  • Tom Kha Gai – Coconut milk soup with chicken and galangal.
  • Gaeng Jued – Clear soup with tofu, vegetables, and minced pork.

🍚 Rice Dishes

  • Khao Pad – Thai fried rice with egg, vegetables, and meat.
  • Khao Man Gai – Thai chicken rice with flavorful broth and sauce.
  • Khao Niew Moo Ping – Grilled pork skewers with sticky rice.
  • Khao Niew Mamuang – Mango sticky rice with coconut milk (dessert).

🍢 Street Food Favorites

  • Satay – Marinated grilled meat skewers served with peanut sauce.
  • Hoy Tod – Crispy oyster pancake with chili sauce.
  • Kai Jeow – Thai omelet served over jasmine rice.
  • Moo Tod – Fried marinated pork, crispy and savory.

These dishes remind me of home and are perfect for sharing with friends and family. What's your favorite Thai dish?
