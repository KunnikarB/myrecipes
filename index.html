        The best Thai cooking uses the freshest
        ingredients available to create the unique Thai taste. This taste can be
        defined as the use of all 5 flavors:spicy, sweet, salty, bitter and sour. 
        
        Only Thai cuisine brings out all of these flavors
        to play together harmoniously in a meal. 
        
        Cooking Thai food should be fun! Cooking can be meditative, relaxing and enjoyable. 
        Especially when the recipes are not complex and turn out tasty.
